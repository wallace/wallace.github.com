Resque-lonely job

Jan 28th, 2013

A new version of resque-lonely_job has been released!

Way back in June, I developed a plugin for resque. Back then, resque was horribly unmaintained with hundreds of issues and numerous pull requests. The internal API was a mess but a few people got the ball rolling and have straightened the project out for the most part.

At the time, I wasn’t able to implement all the features that I wanted but just enough to cover the basics. Since then, a nice, kind soul has come along and implemented the features they needed. A huge thank you to Tatsuhiko Miyagawa for his pull request.

I’m happy to release version 0.0.4 of resque-lonely_job. Enjoy!
